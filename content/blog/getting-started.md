# Getting started with Nuxt Content

Nuxt Content turns Markdown files into typed, queryable pages. Write in your editor — or in Studio — and ship.

## Collections

Group related documents into collections so listings and detail pages stay simple.

## Studio

Edit posts visually in development. Changes sync straight to your filesystem.
